About Us

Seek is an innovative academic search engine built with unique ideas by Saravanan and Sukirthan. Our mission is to provide a simple and direct way to search for academic articles, making it easier for students, researchers, and academics to find the information they need.

Why Choose Seek?

At Seek, we understand the challenges of finding quality academic resources. Our search engine is designed to filter through vast amounts of data to bring you the most relevant results quickly and efficiently. Whether you're working on a research project, a thesis, or just expanding your knowledge, Seek is here to help.
